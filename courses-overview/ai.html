AI for Business: Applications and Daily Use

Course Overview

The AI for Business course at New Age University introduces the practical application of Artificial Intelligence in daily business operations. This course is designed to help business professionals, entrepreneurs, and managers understand how AI can streamline processes, enhance decision-making, and increase efficiency. From automating routine tasks to applying machine learning in business analytics, this course focuses on real-world AI applications in business environments.

Key Details

  • Duration: 6 months
  • Level: Beginner to Advance
  • Format: Online, Self-paced
  • Start Date:January 13th, 2025
  • Cost: Free

Curriculum

The curriculum for this course includes the following modules:

  • Week 1: Introduction to AI and Its Business Applications - Understanding the fundamentals and potential of AI in the modern business landscape.
  • Week 2: Text Generation and Its Business Applications - Explore text generation technologies and their practical applications.
  • Week 3: Prompt Engineering Basics-Advance - Learn the fundamentals of prompt engineering to advanced techniques and its importance.
  • Week 4: AI for Image, Audio & Video Generation - Explore the potential of AI in visual, audio and video creation.
  • Week 5: Chatbots and Conversational AI- Explores how AI is used to power chatbots and conversational agents, improving customer service and user engagement.
  • Week 6: AI in Coding: Software & Web Development - Explore how AI is transforming the world of software development, offering tools that help automate coding and debugging.
  • Week 7: Integrating AI into Business Workflows - Learn how to seamlessly integrate AI tools into existing business processes for operational improvement and innovation.
  • Week 8: Data Management and Ethics in AI - Address data management practices and ethical considerations when using AI in business.
  • Week 9: Future Trends in AI - Explore emerging trends and future developments in AI, focusing on their potential impact on business.
  • Week 10: Capstone Project Execution - Participants prepare for the capstone project, focusing on project development and presentation skills.
