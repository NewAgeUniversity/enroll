NAU - AI for Business

AI for Business: Applications and Daily Use

Course Overview

The AI for Business course at New Age University introduces the practical application of Artificial Intelligence in daily business operations. This course is designed to help business professionals, entrepreneurs, and managers understand how AI can streamline processes, enhance decision-making, and increase efficiency. From automating routine tasks to applying machine learning in business analytics, this course focuses on real-world AI applications in business environments.

Key Details

  • Duration: 6 weeks
  • Level: Beginner to Intermediate
  • Format: Online, Self-paced
  • Start Date: November 1, 2024
  • Cost: Free

Curriculum

The curriculum for this course includes the following modules:

  • Week 1: Introduction to AI in Business - Understanding the fundamentals and potential of AI in the modern business landscape.
  • Week 2: AI for Task Automation - How AI can be used to automate daily tasks and save time.
  • Week 3: Machine Learning Basics - Understanding machine learning and its applications in predictive analytics for business.
  • Week 4: AI Tools for Business - Exploring AI tools like chatbots, AI-driven CRMs, and workflow automation software.
  • Week 5: AI for Data Analysis - Leveraging AI to enhance business insights and decision-making through advanced analytics.
  • Week 6: AI Strategy for Businesses - Implementing AI solutions in a business and aligning them with your business goals.
