Digital Marketing

Course Overview

The Digital Marketing course at New Age University is designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to navigate the dynamic world of online marketing. This course covers a range of digital marketing strategies and tools, including search engine optimization (SEO), pay-per-click (PPC) advertising, social media marketing, content marketing, and email marketing. Students will learn how to create effective digital marketing campaigns, analyze performance metrics, and drive traffic and conversions for businesses. Ideal for aspiring marketers and business owners, this course provides practical insights and hands-on experience in digital marketing.

Key Details

  • Duration: 6 months
  • Level: Beginner to Advance
  • Format: Online, Self-paced
  • Start Date: January 13th, 2025
  • Cost: Free

Curriculum

The curriculum for this course includes the following modules:

  • Week 2: SEO Fundamentals - Techniques for optimizing websites for search engines, including keyword research, on-page and off-page SEO, and technical SEO basics.
  • Week 3: PPC Advertising - Strategies for creating and managing effective pay-per-click campaigns using platforms like Google Ads and Bing Ads.
  • Week 4: Social Media Marketing - Leveraging social media platforms to engage with audiences, create a social media strategy, and implement social media advertising.
  • Week 5: Content Marketing - Developing content strategies to attract and retain customers, including the creation and distribution of various content types.
  • Week 6: Email Marketing - Crafting and executing email campaigns to nurture leads, engage customers, and drive conversions while measuring campaign performance.
  • Week 7: Analytics and Reporting - Using tools like Google Analytics to measure, analyze, and report on digital marketing performance, focusing on key metrics and data-driven decision-making.
  • Week 8: Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) - Understanding the principles of CRO and techniques for improving conversion rates, including A/B testing and user experience design.
  • Week 9: Influencer Marketing and Affiliate Marketing - Exploring influencer partnerships and affiliate marketing strategies to enhance brand visibility and reach.
  • Week 10: Capstone Project & Future Trends in Digital Marketing - Discussing emerging trends such as AI, marketing automation, voice search optimization, and the role of virtual/augmented reality in digital marketing.
