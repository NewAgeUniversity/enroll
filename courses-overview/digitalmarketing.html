NAU - Digital Marketing

Digital Marketing

Course Overview

The Digital Marketing course at New Age University is designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to navigate the dynamic world of online marketing. This course covers a range of digital marketing strategies and tools, including search engine optimization (SEO), pay-per-click (PPC) advertising, social media marketing, content marketing, and email marketing. Students will learn how to create effective digital marketing campaigns, analyze performance metrics, and drive traffic and conversions for businesses. Ideal for aspiring marketers and business owners, this course provides practical insights and hands-on experience in digital marketing.

Key Details

  • Duration: 8 weeks
  • Level: Beginner to Intermediate
  • Format: Online, Self-paced
  • Start Date: December 1, 2025
  • Cost: Free

Curriculum

The curriculum for this course includes the following modules:

  • Week 1: Introduction to Digital Marketing - Overview of digital marketing channels and strategies.
  • Week 2: SEO Fundamentals - Techniques for optimizing websites for search engines and improving rankings.
  • Week 3: PPC Advertising - Strategies for creating and managing effective pay-per-click campaigns.
  • Week 4: Social Media Marketing - Leveraging social media platforms to engage with audiences and drive traffic.
  • Week 5: Content Marketing - Developing content strategies to attract and retain customers.
  • Week 6: Email Marketing - Crafting and executing email campaigns to nurture leads and drive conversions.
  • Week 7: Analytics and Reporting - Using tools to measure and analyze digital marketing performance.
  • Week 8: Final Project - Developing a comprehensive digital marketing strategy for a real or hypothetical business.
