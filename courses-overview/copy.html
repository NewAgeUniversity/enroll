NAU - Copywriting

Copywriting

Course Overview

The Copywriting course at New Age University focuses on developing persuasive writing skills essential for creating effective marketing and advertising content. This course covers the principles of writing compelling copy for various media, including websites, advertisements, social media, and email campaigns. Students will learn techniques for engaging audiences, crafting impactful messages, and optimizing copy for conversions. Ideal for aspiring copywriters and marketing professionals, this course provides practical experience and valuable insights into the art of persuasive writing.

Key Details

  • Duration: 8 weeks
  • Level: Beginner to Intermediate
  • Format: Online, Self-paced
  • Start Date: August 1, 2025
  • Cost: Free

Curriculum

The curriculum for this course includes the following modules:

  • Week 1: Introduction to Copywriting - Understanding the role of copywriting in marketing.
  • Week 2: Writing for Different Media - Techniques for crafting copy for web, print, and social media.
  • Week 3: Persuasive Techniques - Methods for creating compelling and persuasive messages.
  • Week 4: Headlines and Taglines - Crafting attention-grabbing headlines and memorable taglines.
  • Week 5: Crafting Calls to Action - Developing effective calls to action that drive conversions.
  • Week 6: SEO Copywriting - Integrating keywords and optimizing copy for search engines.
  • Week 7: Editing and Refining - Techniques for polishing and improving your copy.
  • Week 8: Final Project - Creating a complete copywriting campaign for a real or hypothetical product.
