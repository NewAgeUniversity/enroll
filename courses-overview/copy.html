Copywriting

Course Overview

The Copywriting course at New Age University focuses on developing persuasive writing skills essential for creating effective marketing and advertising content. This course covers the principles of writing compelling copy for various media, including websites, advertisements, social media, and email campaigns. Students will learn techniques for engaging audiences, crafting impactful messages, and optimizing copy for conversions. Ideal for aspiring copywriters and marketing professionals, this course provides practical experience and valuable insights into the art of persuasive writing.

Key Details

  • Duration: 6 months
  • Level: Beginner to Advance
  • Format: Online, Self-paced
  • Start Date: (Commig Soon!) July 13th, 2025
  • Cost: Free

Curriculum

This 10-week course is designed to guide participants from beginner to advanced copywriting skills, equipping them with practical tools and techniques for effective copy creation across various media. Each week includes targeted topics, assignments, and hands-on practice, culminating in a capstone project in Week 10.

  • Week 1: Introduction to Copywriting - Understanding the fundamentals, the role of copywriting in marketing, and core principles of effective copy.
  • Week 2: Writing for Different Media - Techniques for crafting copy for websites, social media, email, and print, with a focus on adjusting style and tone for each medium.
  • Week 3: Persuasive Writing Techniques - Methods for creating compelling messages, including psychological triggers and storytelling techniques that drive engagement.
  • Week 4: Crafting Headlines and Taglines - Strategies for writing attention-grabbing headlines and memorable taglines that encapsulate brand messaging.
  • Week 5: Calls to Action (CTAs) - Developing impactful calls to action that encourage desired responses and drive conversions.
  • Week 6: SEO Copywriting Basics - Integrating keywords naturally, optimizing copy for search engines, and balancing readability with SEO requirements.
  • Week 7: Copywriting for Ads - Exploring ad copy for digital and print platforms, focusing on clarity, brevity, and persuasive appeal in ad formats.
  • Week 8: Editing and Refining - Techniques for editing and polishing copy, including grammar, style consistency, and flow improvements.
  • Week 9: Advanced Copywriting Techniques - Exploring complex strategies like conversion rate optimization, A/B testing, and advanced storytelling for seasoned copywriters.
  • Week 10: Capstone Project - Creating a complete copywriting campaign for a real or hypothetical product, integrating all skills learned throughout the course.
