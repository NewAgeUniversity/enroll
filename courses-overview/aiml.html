NAU - AI(machine Learning)

AI & Machine Learning

Course Overview

The AI & Machine Learning course at New Age University is designed for individuals seeking to explore the world of artificial intelligence and machine learning. This course provides a solid introduction to AI concepts, algorithms, and real-world applications. Participants will learn key techniques such as supervised and unsupervised learning, neural networks, and data processing. By the end of this course, students will be equipped to build AI models and understand how AI is transforming industries.

Key Details

  • Duration: 6 months
  • Level: Beginner to Advance
  • Format: Online, Self-paced
  • Start Date:January 13th, 2025
  • Cost: Free

Curriculum

The curriculum for this course includes the following modules:

  • Week 1: Introduction to AI - Overview of AI, its history, and future trends.
  • Week 2: Machine Learning Fundamentals - Key ML concepts, algorithms, and use cases.
  • Week 3: Supervised Learning - Understanding supervised learning techniques such as regression and classification.
  • Week 4: Unsupervised Learning - Clustering, dimensionality reduction, and anomaly detection.
  • Week 5: Neural Networks & Deep Learning - Introduction to neural networks and their applications.
  • Week 6: Data Processing & Feature Engineering - Techniques to process and prepare data for ML models.
  • Week 7: Model Evaluation & Validation - Best practices for evaluating and fine-tuning AI models.
  • Week 8: AI in Industry - How AI is applied in healthcare, finance, and other sectors.
  • Week 9: Ethical AI & Bias - Exploring the ethical implications of AI and addressing bias in models.
  • Week 10: Natural Language Processing - Introduction to NLP and its real-world applications.
  • Week 11: Computer Vision - Fundamentals of computer vision and image processing.
  • Week 12: Building AI Projects - Hands-on AI project development to apply learning.
