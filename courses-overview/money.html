Money & Financial Intelligence

Course Overview

The Money & Financial Intelligence course at New Age University is designed to equip individuals with essential financial knowledge to make informed decisions. This course covers topics such as personal finance, investing, budgeting, and understanding financial markets. It is ideal for anyone looking to improve their financial literacy, whether for personal or business purposes. By the end of the course, students will have the tools to effectively manage money, invest wisely, and achieve financial stability.

Key Details

  • Duration: 10 weeks
  • Level: Beginner to Intermediate
  • Format: Online, Self-paced
  • Start Date: November 15, 2024
  • Cost: Free

Curriculum

The curriculum for this course includes the following modules:

  • Week 1: Introduction to Financial Literacy - The basics of money management and its importance.
  • Week 2: Budgeting and Saving - Practical steps for creating and managing a personal budget.
  • Week 3: Understanding Credit - How to manage credit, debt, and improve your credit score.
  • Week 4: Investment Fundamentals - Introduction to stocks, bonds, mutual funds, and other investment vehicles.
  • Week 5: Risk Management & Insurance - How to protect yourself financially through insurance and managing risks.
  • Week 6: Retirement Planning - Strategies to prepare for a financially secure retirement.
  • Week 7: Financial Markets - Understanding how the stock market, forex, and other financial markets operate.
  • Week 8: Tax Planning - Basics of tax planning and how to reduce tax liabilities.
  • Week 9: Building Wealth - Effective strategies for accumulating and growing wealth over time.
  • Week 10: Financial Independence - Achieving financial freedom through smart money management and investments.
