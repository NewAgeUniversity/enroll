Sustainable Development and Green Technologies

Course Overview

The Sustainable Development and Green Technologies course at New Age University provides a comprehensive understanding of sustainable practices and innovative green technologies. This course covers the principles of sustainability, including environmental, economic, and social dimensions, and explores cutting-edge technologies designed to promote ecological balance and reduce environmental impact. Students will learn about renewable energy sources, sustainable materials, and strategies for implementing eco-friendly practices in various industries. Ideal for professionals and enthusiasts aiming to contribute to a greener future, this course offers practical insights and actionable knowledge on sustainable development.

Key Details

  • Duration: 8 weeks
  • Level: Beginner to Intermediate
  • Format: Online, Self-paced
  • Start Date: March 1, 2026
  • Cost: Free

Curriculum

The curriculum for this course includes the following modules:

  • Week 1: Introduction to Sustainability - Principles and goals of sustainable development.
  • Week 2: Renewable Energy Sources - Overview of solar, wind, hydro, and other renewable energy technologies.
  • Week 3: Sustainable Materials - Study of eco-friendly materials and their applications in construction and manufacturing.
  • Week 4: Green Technologies - Innovations in technology aimed at reducing environmental impact.
  • Week 5: Environmental Impact Assessment - Techniques for evaluating and minimizing the environmental impact of projects.
  • Week 6: Circular Economy - Concepts of recycling, reuse, and reducing waste in a circular economic model.
  • Week 7: Implementing Sustainable Practices - Strategies for integrating sustainability into business operations and everyday life.
  • Week 8: Final Project - Developing a sustainable project plan or strategy for a real or hypothetical scenario.
