Business Consultation

Course Overview

The Business Consultation course at New Age University is designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to provide expert advice to businesses seeking to improve their performance, solve problems, and achieve their strategic goals. This course covers the essential aspects of business consulting, including needs assessment, problem-solving techniques, and developing actionable recommendations. Ideal for aspiring consultants and business advisors, this course provides practical insights and tools to help clients navigate challenges and drive growth.

Key Details

  • Duration: 8 weeks
  • Level: Intermediate
  • Format: Online, Self-paced
  • Start Date: October 1, 2025
  • Cost: Free

Curriculum

The curriculum for this course includes the following modules:

  • Week 1: Introduction to Business Consulting - Overview of consulting roles and methodologies.
  • Week 2: Needs Assessment - Techniques for identifying client needs and business problems.
  • Week 3: Data Collection and Analysis - Methods for gathering and analyzing data to inform recommendations.
  • Week 4: Developing Recommendations - Crafting actionable and strategic recommendations for clients.
  • Week 5: Implementation Planning - Creating plans to help clients implement recommendations effectively.
  • Week 6: Communication Skills - Enhancing communication skills for effective consulting and client interactions.
  • Week 7: Managing Client Relationships - Strategies for building and maintaining strong client relationships.
  • Week 8: Final Project - Conducting a comprehensive consulting project for a hypothetical or real client.
