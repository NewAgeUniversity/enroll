NAU - Video Editing

Video Editing

Course Overview

The Video Editing course at New Age University is designed to provide students with the skills and techniques needed to create compelling and professional video content. This course covers everything from the basics of video editing software to advanced techniques for editing, color grading, and audio enhancement. Participants will learn how to use industry-standard tools to craft engaging videos for various platforms and purposes, whether for personal projects, social media, or professional production.

Key Details

  • Duration: 6 months
  • Level: Beginner to Advanced
  • Format: Online, Self-paced
  • Start Date:January 13th, 2025
  • Cost: Free

Curriculum

The curriculum for this course includes the following modules:

  • Week 1: Introduction to Video Editing - Overview of video editing principles and software.
  • Week 2: Basic Editing Techniques - Cutting, trimming, and arranging video clips.
  • Week 3: Advanced Editing - Using effects, transitions, and keyframes.
  • Week 4: Color Grading - Techniques for adjusting color and enhancing visual appeal.
  • Week 5: Audio Editing - Integrating and optimizing audio for video projects.
  • Week 6: Motion Graphics - Adding and animating graphics within your videos.
  • Week 7: Exporting and Publishing - Best practices for exporting and sharing videos on different platforms.
  • Week 8: Final Project - Applying learned techniques to create a complete video project.
