NAU - Content Development

Content Development

Course Overview

The Content Development course at New Age University is crafted to provide students with the essential skills and techniques needed to create engaging and effective content across various platforms. This course covers the entire content creation process, from ideation and research to writing and editing. Students will learn how to develop content strategies, optimize for SEO, and tailor content to meet audience needs and business objectives. Ideal for content creators, marketers, and anyone involved in producing digital content, this course offers practical insights and hands-on experience.

Key Details

  • Duration: 8 weeks
  • Level: Beginner to Intermediate
  • Format: Online, Self-paced
  • Start Date: November 1, 2025
  • Cost: Free

Curriculum

The curriculum for this course includes the following modules:

  • Week 1: Introduction to Content Development - Understanding content types and the creation process.
  • Week 2: Content Strategy - Developing strategies for effective content planning and distribution.
  • Week 3: Research and Ideation - Techniques for researching topics and generating content ideas.
  • Week 4: Writing and Editing - Best practices for writing, editing, and proofreading content.
  • Week 5: SEO Optimization - Strategies for optimizing content for search engines and improving visibility.
  • Week 6: Visual Content - Creating and incorporating visual elements to enhance content engagement.
  • Week 7: Content Distribution - Methods for distributing and promoting content across various channels.
  • Week 8: Final Project - Developing a comprehensive content plan and executing a content piece for a real or hypothetical project.
