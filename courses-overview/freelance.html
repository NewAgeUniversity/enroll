Freelance Fundamentals

Course Overview

The Freelance Fundamentals course at New Age University is designed to equip aspiring freelancers with the essential skills and knowledge needed to start and succeed in the freelance industry. This course covers key aspects of freelancing, including finding clients, managing projects, setting rates, and building a personal brand. Participants will gain practical insights into establishing a successful freelance career, managing time effectively, and navigating common challenges faced by freelancers.

Key Details

  • Duration: 6 months
  • Level: Beginner to Advance
  • Format: Online, Self-paced
  • Start Date:(Commig Soon!) July 13th, 2025
  • Cost: Free

Curriculum

The curriculum for this course includes the following modules:

  • Week 1: Introduction to Freelancing - Overview of the freelance industry, its opportunities, and the mindset needed for success.
  • Week 2: Finding Clients - Strategies for identifying, attracting, and retaining potential clients through networking and online platforms.
  • Week 3: Setting Rates and Contracts - How to determine your pricing structure, create effective contracts, and negotiate terms with clients.
  • Week 4: Project Management - Tools and techniques for managing freelance projects efficiently, including time management and organization skills.
  • Week 5: Building a Personal Brand - Techniques for establishing, promoting, and differentiating your freelance brand in a competitive market.
  • Week 6: Marketing Your Services - Effective marketing strategies, including social media, content marketing, and online portfolios to showcase your work.
  • Week 7: Financial Management for Freelancers - Basics of budgeting, managing income and expenses, and understanding taxes and legal obligations.
  • Week 8: Navigating Challenges - Common challenges in freelancing (e.g., client disputes, isolation) and strategies to overcome them for long-term success.
  • Week 9: Leveraging Technology - Tools and platforms that can enhance productivity, communication, and collaboration with clients and other freelancers.
  • Week 10: Capstone Project - Develop a comprehensive freelance business plan, including client acquisition strategies, marketing plans, and a portfolio of work, to showcase your freelance skills and readiness.

Gain essential freelancing skills from client acquisition to project management, culminating in a hands-on capstone project that prepares you for the freelance marketplace.
