Freelance Fundamentals

Course Overview

The Freelance Fundamentals course at New Age University is designed to equip aspiring freelancers with the essential skills and knowledge needed to start and succeed in the freelance industry. This course covers key aspects of freelancing, including finding clients, managing projects, setting rates, and building a personal brand. Participants will gain practical insights into establishing a successful freelance career, managing time effectively, and navigating common challenges faced by freelancers.

Key Details

  • Duration: 6 weeks
  • Level: Beginner
  • Format: Online, Self-paced
  • Start Date: March 10, 2025
  • Cost: Free

Curriculum

The curriculum for this course includes the following modules:

  • Week 1: Introduction to Freelancing - Overview of the freelance industry and its opportunities.
  • Week 2: Finding Clients - Strategies for identifying and attracting potential clients.
  • Week 3: Setting Rates and Contracts - How to price your services and create effective contracts.
  • Week 4: Project Management - Tools and techniques for managing freelance projects efficiently.
  • Week 5: Building a Personal Brand - Techniques for establishing and promoting your freelance brand.
  • Week 6: Navigating Challenges - Common challenges in freelancing and strategies to overcome them.
