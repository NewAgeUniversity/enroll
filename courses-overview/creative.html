NAU - Creative Writing

Creative Writing

Course Overview

The Creative Writing course at New Age University is designed to inspire and develop your writing skills across various genres. This course explores techniques for crafting compelling narratives, building characters, and mastering dialogue. Participants will engage in writing exercises, receive feedback on their work, and learn how to revise and polish their writing. Whether you're an aspiring author, a blogger, or someone who wants to express their creativity through words, this course will help you refine your voice and style.

Key Details

  • Duration: 8 weeks
  • Level: Beginner to Intermediate
  • Format: Online, Self-paced
  • Start Date: June 1, 2025
  • Cost: Free

Curriculum

The curriculum for this course includes the following modules:

  • Week 1: Introduction to Creative Writing - Fundamentals of storytelling and writing.
  • Week 2: Developing Characters - Techniques for creating and developing memorable characters.
  • Week 3: Crafting Dialogue - Writing realistic and engaging dialogue.
  • Week 4: Plot and Structure - Structuring your narrative and developing plotlines.
  • Week 5: Setting and World Building - Creating immersive settings and worlds for your stories.
  • Week 6: Point of View and Voice - Exploring different narrative perspectives and writing styles.
  • Week 7: Revising and Editing - Techniques for revising and polishing your writing.
  • Week 8: Final Project - Completing and presenting a polished piece of creative writing.
