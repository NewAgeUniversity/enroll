MS Office Mastery

Course Overview

The MS Office Mastery course at New Age University provides a comprehensive guide to mastering Microsoft Office applications, including Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook. This course is designed for individuals seeking to enhance their productivity and efficiency in professional and personal settings. Participants will gain hands-on experience with essential tools and features, learn advanced techniques, and develop skills to streamline their workflow and create professional documents, presentations, and spreadsheets.

Key Details

  • Duration: 6 months
  • Level: Beginner to Advanced
  • Format: Online, Self-paced
  • Start Date: (Commig Soon!) July 13th, 2025
  • Cost: Free

Curriculum

The curriculum for this course includes the following modules:

  • Week 1: Introduction to MS Office - Overview of the MS Office suite and its applications.
  • Week 2: Microsoft Word - Basic and advanced features for document creation and formatting.
  • Week 3: Microsoft Excel - Data entry, formulas, and advanced functions for spreadsheet management.
  • Week 4: Microsoft PowerPoint - Creating and designing impactful presentations.
  • Week 5: Microsoft Outlook - Managing emails, calendar, and tasks effectively.
  • Week 6: Integration Techniques - How to integrate MS Office applications for improved workflow.
  • Week 7: Collaboration Tools - Utilizing MS Office features for teamwork and collaboration.
  • Week 8: Advanced Data Management - Utilizing advanced features in Excel for data analysis.
  • Week 9: Troubleshooting and Tips - Common issues and tips for efficient use of MS Office tools.
  • Week 10: Final Project - Application of skills learned in a comprehensive project.
