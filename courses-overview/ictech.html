Introduction to Computer & Technology

Course Overview

The Introduction to Computer & Technology course at New Age University is designed to give beginners a comprehensive understanding of the fundamentals of computers and technology. This course covers key concepts such as hardware, software, operating systems, networking, and the impact of technology on society. Ideal for those new to the tech field or those looking to refresh their foundational knowledge, this course sets the stage for more advanced study in IT and computer sciences.

Key Details

  • Duration: 6 months
  • Level: Beginner to Advance
  • Format: Online, Self-paced
  • Start Date: (Commig Soon!) July 13th, 2025
  • Cost: Free

Curriculum

The curriculum for this Introduction to Computers and Technology course includes the following modules:

  • Week 1: Introduction to Computers - Overview of computer systems and technology evolution.
  • Week 2: Hardware & Software Basics - Understanding computer components and their functions.
  • Week 3: Operating Systems - Exploring operating systems like Windows, macOS, and Linux.
  • Week 4: Networking & The Internet - Basics of networking and how the internet operates.
  • Week 5: Data Management - Introduction to databases, data storage, and retrieval techniques.
  • Week 6: Cybersecurity Fundamentals - Understanding security risks and best practices for protection.
  • Week 7: Digital Communication Tools - Overview of email, messaging, and collaboration platforms.
  • Week 8: Cloud Computing - Exploring cloud services, storage, and applications.
  • Week 9: Basic Troubleshooting & Maintenance - Common issues and how to maintain computer systems.
  • Week 10: Future Trends in Technology - Discussing emerging technologies and their potential impact.
