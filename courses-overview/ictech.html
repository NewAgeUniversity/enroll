NAU - Intro to Computer & Technology

Introduction to Computer & Technology

Course Overview

The Introduction to Computer & Technology course at New Age University is designed to give beginners a comprehensive understanding of the fundamentals of computers and technology. This course covers key concepts such as hardware, software, operating systems, networking, and the impact of technology on society. Ideal for those new to the tech field or those looking to refresh their foundational knowledge, this course sets the stage for more advanced study in IT and computer sciences.

Key Details

  • Duration: 4 weeks
  • Level: Beginner
  • Format: Online, Self-paced
  • Start Date: October 10, 2024
  • Cost: Free

Curriculum

The curriculum for this course includes the following modules:

  • Week 1: Introduction to Computers - Overview of computer systems, history, and evolution of technology.
  • Week 2: Hardware & Software Basics - Understanding the components of a computer, including both hardware and software.
  • Week 3: Operating Systems - A deep dive into operating systems like Windows, macOS, and Linux.
  • Week 4: Networking & The Internet - Basics of networking, how the internet works, and its impact on the modern world.
