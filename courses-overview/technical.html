NAU - Technical Writing

Technical Writing

Course Overview

The Technical Writing course at New Age University is designed to equip students with the skills needed to produce clear, concise, and effective technical documents. This course covers the fundamentals of technical writing, including how to write manuals, reports, and user guides. Students will learn best practices for organizing content, using technical terminology, and creating documentation that is both accessible and accurate. Ideal for those aiming to work in technical fields or improve their documentation skills, this course provides a solid foundation in technical communication.

Key Details

  • Duration: 6 months
  • Level: Beginner to Advance
  • Format: Online, Self-paced
  • Start Date: January 13th, 2025
  • Cost: Free

Curriculum

The curriculum for this course includes the following modules:

  • Week 1: Introduction to Technical Writing - Basics of technical writing and its importance.
  • Week 2: Writing Clear and Concise Content - Techniques for clear communication and concise writing.
  • Week 3: Document Structure and Design - Organizing and formatting technical documents for readability.
  • Week 4: Technical Terminology - Understanding and using technical language appropriately.
  • Week 5: Manuals and User Guides - Creating effective manuals and user guides.
  • Week 6: Reports and Proposals - Writing detailed reports and proposals for technical audiences.
  • Week 7: Editing and Proofreading - Techniques for reviewing and improving technical documents.
  • Week 8: Final Project - Developing and presenting a comprehensive technical document.
