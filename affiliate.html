Our Partners & Affiliates

Empowering Education Through Strategic Partnerships

At New Age University (NAU), we believe in the power of collaboration. By partnering with forward-thinking organizations, we bring the best opportunities to our students, helping them excel in their careers and contribute to the future of innovation.

Key Partner: ABION Industries

About ABION Industries:

ABION Industries is a pioneering startup conglomerate founded in 2025 by the ABION brothers. With a vision to foster innovation, cultural pride, and sustainable development, ABION operates in multiple sectors, including Entertainment, Technology, Education, Space Exploration, Health, Agriculture, and many more.

How ABION Industries Supports NAU:

  • Advanced learning tools (AI-driven educational platforms)
  • Internship placements across ABION subsidiaries
  • Entrepreneurship and startup mentorship programs
  • Access to proprietary ABION software and tools (e.g., AnalytiX, Bible AI)

ABION Industries Subsidiaries Collaborating with NAU:

  • ABION Education: Supports curriculum development and certification.
  • ABION Technologies: Provides tech solutions and digital learning platforms.
  • ABION Entertainment: Involved in creative digital media internships.
  • ABION Finance: Offers mentorship in financial literacy and entrepreneurship.
  • ABION Health & Wellness: Provides wellness programs and online health courses.

Why Partner with NAU?

By partnering with New Age University, organizations gain access to a pool of talented, motivated learners equipped with the skills needed to thrive in the digital and business landscape.

  • Collaborative Growth: Help shape the next generation of leaders through mentorship and practical learning.
  • Brand Exposure: Your brand will be featured on our platforms, reaching students, educators, and professionals worldwide.
  • Tailored Programs: Work with us to design specialized courses or internship programs that meet your industry needs.
  • Access to Talent: Partner organizations will have the first opportunity to hire or collaborate with graduates of NAU.
