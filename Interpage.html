Welcome to the NAU Internship Program

Practical experience, skill development, and career advancement opportunities await you.

Internship Overview

Duration: 3 months

Location: Remote

Eligibility: Open to students enrolled in NAU courses or individuals seeking experience in relevant fields.

Why Join Our Internship Program?

  • Practical Experience: Work on real projects that impact our organization and the community.
  • Skill Development: Enhance your skills in areas such as project management, digital marketing, content creation, and more.
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with industry professionals and expand your professional network.
  • Career Advancement: Gain valuable experience to boost your resume and career prospects.
  • Mentorship: Receive guidance and support from experienced professionals in your field.

Available Internship Positions

Explore various internship opportunities tailored to your skills and interests.

Application Process

  1. Submit Your Application: Fill out the Internship Application Form.
  2. Resume and Cover Letter: Include a resume and cover letter outlining your interest and qualifications.
  3. Interview: Selected candidates will be contacted for an interview.
  4. Offer Letter: Successful candidates will receive an offer letter with details regarding the internship.

Internship Timeline

Application Period: Once every quarter

Interview Dates: Relative to application

Internship Start Date: Relative to acceptance

Internship End Date: Relative to Start date
